Hey! 👋 Here's the study tool for our Computer Network & Security module. Everything you need is in ONE file.

1️⃣ Save the file "study_hub.html" to your phone or PC
2️⃣ Open it in Chrome, Edge or Firefox

🗂️ *WHAT'S INSIDE:*

🗺️ *THE JOURNEY* - See how ALL 19 topics connect in 5 layers. From building the machine → connecting to the network → protecting it all. Click any topic to jump straight to it. This is the KEY to truly understanding the module.
